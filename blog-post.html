How Nua's AI-First Approach Changes Everything

Nua combines deep security expertise with cutting-edge AI to deliver continuous, automated penetration testing that integrates directly into your CI/CD pipeline. Our digital employees think like expert pentesters but operate at machine speed.

  • Continuous testing — No more waiting for quarterly assessments. Nua runs continuously, catching vulnerabilities as code changes.
  • AI-powered prioritization — Not all vulnerabilities are equal. Nua's AI evaluates business context, exploitability, and real-world impact to prioritize what matters most.
  • Seamless integration — Plug into your existing DevOps workflow with native integrations for GitHub, GitLab, Jenkins, and more.
  • Human-quality reporting — Every finding comes with detailed remediation guidance written at the level of a senior security consultant.

Real-World Use Case: Mid-Market Company Without A Dedicated Red Team

A mid-sized SaaS company integrated Nua into their CI/CD pipeline. Within weeks, they identified critical misconfigurations missed by manual audits. With automated testing running continuously, they now catch vulnerabilities as code changes—without waiting for the next quarterly assessment.

Getting Started With Nua

Getting started is straightforward. Our onboarding team works with your security and DevOps leads to configure Nua for your environment. Most teams are up and running within a day—seeing their first automated pentest results within hours.

  1. Connect your infrastructure — link your cloud accounts, repositories, and CI/CD pipelines.
  2. Configure scope — define what's in-scope and set testing parameters.
  3. Launch your first assessment — Nua begins testing immediately and delivers findings in real-time.
  4. Remediate and track — use the dashboard to assign, track, and verify fixes.
