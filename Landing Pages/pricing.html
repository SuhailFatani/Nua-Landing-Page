Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the SHAX ON-DEMAND plans?

Both the Plus and Premium plans include deploy on-demand capabilities, audit-ready reports within 5 days, instant re-testing with automated verification, frictionless authentication testing (2FA, Magic Link, Email), detailed proof-of-concept exploits, actionable remediation guidance, and support for Blackbox, Whitebox, or Greybox testing methodologies.

How does the per-test pricing work?

The Plus ($3,500) and Premium ($7,000) plans are priced per individual penetration test engagement. You pay for each test you commission — there is no subscription or recurring fee. The Plus plan provides the equivalent depth of a 2-week manual test, while Premium matches a 4-week engagement.

What compliance frameworks are covered in the reports?

All Nua penetration test reports are compliance-ready and cover 40+ frameworks including SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, and GDPR. Reports are structured to satisfy auditor requirements and can be submitted directly to regulators, clients, or third-party assessors.

Can I upgrade from Plus to Premium after starting?

Yes. If your application's scope or complexity increases and you need deeper coverage, you can commission a new Premium test at any time. Our team can also advise you on which plan best fits your application before you start — contact us to discuss your specific needs.

What makes the Enterprise plan different from the on-demand plans?

The Enterprise plan goes beyond one-time testing to provide continuous security hardening. It includes ongoing access to the XBOW platform, real-time streaming of findings, vulnerability coverage maps, team collaboration features (multi-member access, shared assessment knowledge), human-directed operatives, and enterprise controls like SSO and API integration — designed for organizations that need persistent security assurance across a broad application portfolio.
